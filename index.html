About me

I am currently doing a PhD in computer science in the ICPS team of the ICube laboratory.

I work on compiler optimizations and speculative runtime code transformations, more specifically on the subject of multi-versioning. That means, selecting the best performing version of a given program in a given execution context.

I like computers, music (especially of the metal variety), singing karaoke and beer.

I'm the guitar player in a small french thrash metal band called Kernel Panik (I am very happy with that name). You can find our music here and follow us on Instagram here.
